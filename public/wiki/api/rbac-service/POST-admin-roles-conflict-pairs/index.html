POST /admin/roles/conflict-pairs

Create a new Separation of Duties (SoD) conflict pair, specifying two mutually exclusive roles (e.g., approver and operator cannot be the same person). After creation, the system automatically detects and prevents conflicts during role assignment. References: NIST SP 800-53 AC-5 (Separation of Duties).

RBAC - SoD `bearerAuth` application/json
